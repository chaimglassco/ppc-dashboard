## Before making changes

- [ ] Confirm the reporting window and attribution delay.
- [ ] Check inventory, Buy Box, suppressed listings, and price changes.
- [ ] Annotate promotions, deal days, and material listing edits.
- [ ] Compare actual spend with the weekly budget plan.

## Control urgent waste

- [ ] Find campaigns with abnormal spend spikes.
- [ ] Review irrelevant search terms for negatives.
- [ ] Reduce bids where CPC exceeds viable economics.
- [ ] Check budget rules and placement multipliers for accidental changes.

## Protect profitable volume

- [ ] Identify profitable campaigns limited by budget.
- [ ] Review lost sales from stock or Buy Box problems.
- [ ] Confirm proven exact terms are not blocked by negatives.
- [ ] Raise bids only where incremental traffic is likely to remain profitable.

## Harvest and route demand

- [ ] Move proven discovery queries into controlled exact targets.
- [ ] Add negative exact routing where duplicate exposure is material.
- [ ] Add converting ASINs to the appropriate product-targeting layer.
- [ ] Update the keyword or target source record.

## Review by horizon

| Horizon | Question | Typical action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 7 days | Is spend pacing safely? | Budget and urgent waste control |
| 30 days | Are targets converting efficiently? | Bid and query decisions |
| 90 days | Is structure supporting growth? | Consolidate or restructure |

## Finish the weekly cycle

- [ ] Record material changes with reasons.
- [ ] Flag retail-readiness blockers for the owner.
- [ ] Write a three-sentence performance summary.
- [ ] Schedule follow-up dates for experiments.
- [ ] Avoid changing the same target again before evidence matures.
